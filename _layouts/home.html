Redefining and redesigning the VA digital experience

Who we are

DS@VA is a new team of some of the nation’s best technologists that aims to improve and simplify the digital experience that Veterans have with VA.

Across sectors, digital technologies have become the standard for service delivery and engagement. DS@VA will bring these industry standards and top technology talent into the VA to help build a 21st century agency that is centered on the needs of Veterans.

Become a Founding Member

Join a quickly growing team that is committed to improving the way government designs and develops digital services. Work alongside world-class engineers and designers from places like Google, Amazon, and Facebook.

A variety of roles are needed, including software engineers, system administrators, designers, and DevOps, with specific interest in those with experience in:

  • Building high-performing engineering teams from scratch
  • Migrating from legacy systems to modern infrastructure
  • Healthcare technology
